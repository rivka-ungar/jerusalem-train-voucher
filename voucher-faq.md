# Jerusalem Train Voucher — FAQ

Consult when: user has questions beyond basic booking.

## What is the voucher?
A free digital pass required for every passenger on the Jerusalem line, in addition to a valid Rav-Kav or ticket. Limits trains to 1,200 passengers for safety.

## How long is it valid?
Only for the specific train it was booked for (date + departure time).

## Can I book for a group?
Yes. Choose the number of vouchers needed (one per passenger). All codes sent to one phone number.

## Cancellation fee?
No. Free cancellation as of April 2026.

## Can I book by phone?
Yes. Call *5770.

## Does it cost money?
No. The voucher is free. Travel ticket purchased separately.

## When did this start?
Introduced for Passover 2026, expected to continue on regular days.

## Source
Israel Railways announcements, March-April 2026.
